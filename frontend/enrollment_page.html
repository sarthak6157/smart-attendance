Set up facial recognition to prevent proxy attendance and speed up check-ins.

Tips for a great capture
  • Use a well-lit area with your face clearly visible.
  • Remove hats, sunglasses, and heavy face coverings.
  • Center your face and look directly at the camera.
  • Hold still for a moment when asked to capture.
  • Ensure only your face is in the frame.
What we do

We securely create a biometric template of your face to verify identity at attendance checkpoints. We do not store raw video frames.
